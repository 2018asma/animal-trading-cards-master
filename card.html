Dog

Cats are similar in anatomy to the other felids, with a strong flexible body, quick reflexes, sharp teeth and retractable claws adapted to killing small prey.

  • Kingdom: Animalia
  • Phylum: Chordata
  • Class: Mammalia
  • Family: Felidae

Cats have a high breeding rate. Under controlled breeding, they can be bred and shown as registered pedigree pets, a hobby known as cat fancy.
